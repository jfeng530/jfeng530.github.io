My name is Jacky Feng

I was first introduced to programming through a class in high school where we used code to teach a robot to perform simple tasks. The entire process resonated with my love for creative problem solving and analytical thinking. After studying mathematics in college, I wanted to explore my creative side. As a result, I transitioned into the field of programming, a field where I find many opportunities to combine my passion for creative endeavours and my background in mathematics.
